Jokes aside...
My name is Hugo.
I like rain,
I like ice cream.
Not much for poetry,
so mistakes were made.

Hanging on,
unsure what to do.
Likes to help,
yet lost like all.
Feel old, so old,
Yet told otherwise,
Time really likes to rub it in.
Yet... I can't really hate it for that.

How annoying...

Goofyness is done,
I hope this went well.
And I wish you all,

Good luck out there.
